The Music of Joan Manuel Serrat

Photo by: Xvazquez (Own work) [Public domain], via Wikimedia Commons

Joan Manuel Serrat is an acclaimed musician, singer-songwriter, recording artist, and performer. He was born in Barcelona, Spain and since the early 1970s he has been considered as one of the most pioneering musicians of modern popular music in Spain and Latin-America. Much of his lyrical style draws its inspiration from many influential Spanish and Latin American poets, such as Federico Garcia Lorca and Rafael Alberti.
